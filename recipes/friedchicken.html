Bolognese Recipe


Description

This crispy fried chicken is golden and delicious, with a flavorful, crunchy coating. Marinated in buttermilk and seasoned with a blend of spices, the chicken is deep-fried to perfection, creating a juicy and tender interior. Perfect for a hearty meal, serve it with sides like mashed potatoes, coleslaw, or cornbread for a true comfort food experience.


  • 4 chicken thighs (or breasts), skin on
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or peanut oil)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the buttermilk with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper. Add the chicken, ensuring it is fully submerged, and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).
  2. In a shallow bowl, mix the flour with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  4. Coat each piece of chicken in the seasoned flour, pressing gently to ensure the coating sticks.
  5. Fry the chicken in batches for 8-12 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through, with an internal temperature of 165째F (75째C).
  6. Drain the fried chicken on paper towels and serve hot with your favorite sides.
