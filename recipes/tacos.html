Bolognese Recipe


Description

These tasty tacos are filled with seasoned ground beef (or chicken) and topped with fresh ingredients like lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa. Wrapped in soft tortillas, they make for a quick and satisfying meal. Customize with your favorite toppings and enjoy a fiesta of flavors in every bite.


  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef (or chicken)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade seasoning blend)
  • 8 soft flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Olive oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the ground beef (or chicken), breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and cooked through.
  2. Drain any excess fat, then add taco seasoning and water (as per package instructions) to the meat. Simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. While the meat cooks, warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until soft and pliable.
  4. To assemble, spoon the seasoned meat onto each tortilla, then top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  5. Fold the tortillas into tacos and serve immediately with your favorite sides like rice or beans.
