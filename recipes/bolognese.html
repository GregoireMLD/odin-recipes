Bolognese Recipe


Description

This classic Bolognese sauce, also known as Rag√π alla Bolognese, is a rich and savory Italian dish made with ground beef, pork, and vegetables like onions, carrots, and celery. Slowly simmered with tomatoes, red wine, and a touch of cream, the sauce develops a deep, comforting flavor that pairs perfectly with pasta. Ideal for a cozy family meal, this hearty sauce is perfect for a variety of dishes, from pasta to lasagna.


  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 300g ground beef
  • 200g ground pork
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion, carrot, and garlic, and cook until softened.
  2. Add the ground beef and pork, and cook until browned, breaking up the meat with a spoon.
  3. Pour in the red wine and let it simmer for a few minutes until it reduces slightly.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, pepper, and herbs. Stir well, and let the sauce simmer on low heat for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in the milk or cream to soften the sauce. Cook for another 5 minutes, then adjust seasoning to taste.
  6. Serve with your favorite pasta or use in lasagna.
